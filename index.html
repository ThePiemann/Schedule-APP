Frequently Asked Questions

Is StudentDash free?expand_more
Yes! StudentDash is 100% free for students. We might add premium themes later, but the core features will always be free.
Can I export my schedule?expand_more
Absolutely. You can export your schedule as a PDF image or as an .ICS file to sync with Google Calendar.
Does it work offline?expand_more
The dashboard caches your schedule, so you can view it offline. Changes will sync once you are back online.
