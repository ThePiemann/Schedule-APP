About Us

StudentDash is an open-source project designed to help students manage their academic lives without the clutter and complexity of traditional tools.

Built by students, for students, our mission is to provide a simple, beautiful, and effective way to track classes, assignments, and exams.

We believe that staying organized shouldn't be a chore. That's why we focused on speed, simplicity, and a distraction-free design. StudentDash is completely free to use. We don't sell your data, and we don't lock features behind a paywall.
