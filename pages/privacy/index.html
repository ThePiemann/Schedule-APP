Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how StudentDash handles your information.

Data Collection

StudentDash is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect personal usage data for advertising purposes. Most of your data, including your schedule and tasks, is stored locally on your device or synced securely if you choose to use cloud features (when available).

Local Storage

We use your browser's Local Storage to save your preferences, theme settings, and cached schedule data for offline access.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for essential functions like authentication (e.g., Google Sign-In). These services have their own privacy policies.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the service implies acceptance of the updated policy.
